Research Interest

I am interested in machine learning for Finance. More specifically, I'm curious to develope faster and more precise methods for option valuation and calibration. In my research, I combine the classical option theory methods with modern methods such as neural networks, tree regression, and differential machine learning to produce powerful tools for trading and risk management. I want to apply these tools to complex, real-world problems, changing the world. In my work I have e.g. developed a new Monte Carlo method to valuate American options using a neural network "Overcoming the Feature Selection Issue in the Pricing of American Options".
